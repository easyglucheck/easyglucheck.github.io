About

EasyGluCheck is a cloud-powered, non-invasive, and cost-effective mobile app based method of continuous blood glucose monitoring for diabetic patients. EasyGluCheck is incredibly accurate with an unprecedented error rate less than 7%, significantly outperforming existing state-of-art non-invasive methods by over 30%. Patients can see long-term trends to optimize their treatments. The app requires no internet connection, increasing platform portability and facility deployment in low-income and rural areas.
